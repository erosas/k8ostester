Storage is grow-only. After deploy you can raise storage size and CNPG expands each PVC online (no downtime) — but only if the StorageClass has allowVolumeExpansion: true (e.g. an AWS EBS gp3 class). You can never shrink it, and EBS limits growth to once every ~6h per volume. Pick a comfortable starting size. Everything else here can be changed after deploy.

App roles for rotation creates two login roles app_a / app_b, both members of the app owner (so they share the data), each from its own secret with a CHANGE-ME placeholder password. That's what the console's Rotate credentials switches between — set real passwords (or rotate immediately) after applying.
Observability. CNPG always exposes metrics; you just need something to scrape them. PodMonitor wires them to a Prometheus Operator; an OTEL endpoint deploys a collector that scrapes and forwards OTLP. The Grafana dashboard, goal waterlines and alerts below only appear once one of those is on — otherwise there's nothing feeding them.
